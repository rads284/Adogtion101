Dogs

The domestic dog a.k.a Canis lupus familiaris is a member of genus Canis. They are the most widely abundant carnivore. Dogs are called Man's best friend, referring to their millennia-long history of close relations, loyalty, and companionship with humans. Before the evolution of wolf into dog, it is posited that humans and wolves worked together hunting game.Dogs perform many roles for people, such as hunting, herding, pulling loads, protection, assisting police and military, companionship and, more recently, aiding handicapped individuals and therapeutic roles.

Rounded head, big and forward-facing eyes, loppy limbs and a teetering gait makes them the most loved pets. Characteristics like big eyes, chubby cheeks and giggling in infants are all designed to encourage others to look after them.
